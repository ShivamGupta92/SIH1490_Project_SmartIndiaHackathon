About Us

Team Victorious

We are Team Victorious, innovators in the field of smart vehicles. Our flagship project, ULV, is an unmanned land vehicle designed to revolutionize the way operations are conducted in difficult terrains and inaccessible areas.

Team Victorious presents ULV, an unmanned ground vehicle designed to operate in difficult terrains and inaccessible areas. Equipped with a visual camera for daytime surveillance, a thermal camera for night-time surveillance, and sonar capabilities for underwater detection, ULV is a versatile and efficient UGV that can operate on any terrain, including water. With the ability to carry a payload of up to 100kg, ULV is suitable for carrying equipment and supplies to remote areas. It is also equipped with various detectors for bomb detection and ground penetrating radar for underground tunnel detection. The 6x6 design of ULV is powered by four electric motors, with regenerative braking, and two propellers for water movement. It has an endurance of 4 hours and comes with a small glider drone for data relay and further surveillance. ULV can be used in various scenarios, such as natural disasters, bomb detection missions, tunnel and infiltration detection at borders, and military applications such as carrying wounded soldiers and extra supplies. ULV can also create a 3D model of the environment and can be used for lake mapping using Bathymetry sonars in small lakes and glacier lakes.
